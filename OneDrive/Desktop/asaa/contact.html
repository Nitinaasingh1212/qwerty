Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about CornerClub

How do I create an event on CornerClub?

+

Creating an event is simple! Click the "Create Event" button in the navigation, fill in your event details, upload images, set your pricing, and publish. You can manage all aspects of your event from your dashboard.

What are the chat rooms feature?

+

Our exclusive chat rooms allow attendees to connect before the event starts. When someone books a ticket, they automatically get access to a private chat room with other attendees. This helps build community and excitement before the event.

How do ticket sales and payments work?

+

We handle all ticket sales securely. Payments are processed through our secure payment gateway, and funds are transferred to your account within 3-5 business days after the event. We charge a small platform fee for paid events.

Can I promote my event on CornerClub?

+

Yes! We offer various promotion options including featuring events on our homepage, email newsletters to relevant subscribers, and social media promotion. You can also use our built-in marketing tools to create shareable event pages.

What safety measures are in place?

+

We verify all event organizers, moderate chat rooms for safety, provide secure payment processing, and offer customer support for any issues. Attendees can also rate and review events to help maintain quality standards.

How do I contact customer support?

+

You can reach our support team via email at cornerclubinn@gmail.com, through our live chat feature, or by calling +91 88849 28295. We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours.
