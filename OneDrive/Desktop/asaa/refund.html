Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

1. Cancellation Policy

Since CornerClub serves as a platform for various event organizers, cancellation policies may vary from event to event. However, the general cancellation terms are as follows:

  • Tickets can be cancelled up to 24 hours before the event start time, unless specified otherwise by the event organizer.
  • Cancellation requests made within 24 hours of the event are generally not eligible for a refund.
  • To cancel a ticket, please contact our support team at cornerclubinn@gmail.com with your Booking ID.

2. Refund Policy

Refunds will be processed to the original payment method within 5-7 business days of the cancellation approval.

  • Event Cancellation: If an event is cancelled by the organizer or CornerClub, a full refund will be issued to all attendees.
  • Event Postponement: If an event is postponed, your ticket will be valid for the new date. If you cannot attend the new date, you may request a refund within 48 hours of the announcement.
  • Processing Fees: Please note that platform fees and payment gateway charges (if any) are non-refundable.

3. Contact for Refunds
